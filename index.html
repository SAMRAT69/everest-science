Exploring the Micro World

Discover the fascinating world beyond the naked eye. Through magnification, we unlock the hidden structures and intricate details of life at a microscopic scale.

Types of Microscopes

  • Laser Microscope

    A laser microscope uses laser light to observe microscopic details with precision.

  • Electron Microscope

    An electron microscope allows scientists to view the smallest structures at atomic levels.
